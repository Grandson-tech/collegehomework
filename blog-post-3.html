What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is using someone else's work or ideas without proper acknowledgment. It is a serious academic offense.

How to Avoid Plagiarism

  • Always cite your sources using the required citation style.
  • Paraphrase information in your own words and still provide credit.
  • Use plagiarism checkers to review your work before submission.

Consequences of Plagiarism

Academic institutions take plagiarism seriously and it can result in penalties, failing grades, or expulsion.

By understanding and avoiding plagiarism, you maintain your academic integrity and produce original work.
